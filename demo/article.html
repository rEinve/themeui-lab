Design System Foundations

Foundations define the system’s visual language and provide shared tokens for color, spacing, typography, motion, and elevation.

Why Token-First Matters

Token-driven styles keep UI behavior predictable and reduce duplication between screens, features, and teams.

When to Use Primitives

Use primitives for layout composition. Keep visual treatment inside components and reserve patterns for page-level assembly.
